LInked In
For hiring managers and other professional contacts, your online presence is often the first way that people may encounter you. LInkedIN is a professional social networking site that many people use as a way to connect with other professionals in their industry. With LinkedIn, you can establish a profile that summarizes your career accomplishments, skills and professional goals. You can also follow specific companies apply to their job posting.
It is common for hiring managers to search job applicants online to a get a better a sense of the candidate and also to uncover any red flags. Thus it is important for job candidates to also be conscious of what information you post about yourself on other social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram etc.
  • Create relevant profiles: don't set up accounts/platforms you won't use
  • Network intentionally and immerse yourself
    • Follow companies you are targeting
    • Consider joining industry or alumni specific forums
  • Be known as a resource: Be thoughtful, share, comment, be active
  • Keep professional exchanges off social sites
  • Do a little at a time, not all at once!
  • Follow companies' social media platforms, not just job search sites
