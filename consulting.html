Entrepreneurship and Consulting
Build a company around your biomedical discoveries and creations. Or take your biomedical skills and apply them to business. Your analytical skills can be used for market research and your scientific knowledge can be applied to technical sales or consulting. The Entrepreneurship and Consulting Career track is for those interested in building and/or supporting businesses and organizations in solving problems, being strategic, and reaching success.
